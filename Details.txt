Propose Your Valentine:
Description:
An application providing a platform to the lovers to propose their loved one by sending a cute, sweet but loving valentine proposal card. So, proposing your lover is so easy with the help of this application. Not just this, a collection of sweet loving card can bring an emotion to your valentine and make the lower rate of rejection.
	
Features:
•	The series of high quality Valentine Proposal cards.
•	Categorized cards to easily classify your party invitation.
•	A User Form to fill out the information about party event.
•	One-Click sending and sharing functionality.
•	Fully Different User Interface Designed keeping in focus point of view of Niche (Target) Market.
•	Live Tiles(Locally & Remotely)
•	Fully Unique Branding (Unique Logo Designing & Graphics Designing)
